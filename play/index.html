How to Play

  • Move your tokens across the board toward the Forum.
  • Curiosity Spaces ask questions — gain knowledge and Perspective.
  • Temple Spaces deepen your understanding and unlock progress.
  • Dialogue Spaces challenge how you respond to others.
  • Wrong answers trigger Conflict — choose how to handle tension.
  • Earn Harmony, Curiosity, and Influence to strengthen your path.
  • Reach the Forum with enough understanding to win.
Win Condition

Reach 10 Influence to win.

To enter the Forum, a player must have at least:

  • 2 Harmony
  • 1 Curiosity

Game Philosophy

This is not a game about proving one belief system right. It is about learning, curiosity, and navigating differences.

Every decision shapes your path — not just what you know, but how you respond to others.

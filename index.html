The Idea

Coexist: The Game began as an original concept in 2012 and is now being thoughtfully revived as a strategic learning game. Players travel through a shared world of harmony, conflict, curiosity, dialogue, and temples of learning on their way to the Forum.

What makes it different

This is not a game about proving one belief system right. It is a game about navigating complexity, collecting perspectives, building harmony, and reaching the Forum through learning and thoughtful play.

How it works

Players move through a dynamic board, answer Curiosity cards, engage in Dialogue, manage Conflict respectfully, collect Perspective Tokens, and build enough influence to enter the Forum.

Game Pillars

The project is built around three foundational ideas that shape every future version of the game.

Strategy

Choose routes, manage resources, collect perspective sets, and time your move into the Forum.

Learning

Gain Perspective Tokens through respectful educational prompts and build a personal map of understanding.

Coexistence

Dialogue and Harmony matter. The game rewards curiosity, listening, and thoughtful interaction.
